WHAT IS NFT SWEEPER ?

NFT SWEEPER is the first online tool that allows, among other things, the exchange of NFTs for NTFs.
You can easily login with your web 3.0 wallet and create a session for you and your friend.
In this session you can easily and safely carry out your exchange.
In the near future your are will be able to trade entire collections.
We are currently in the beta phase.
We are still building!

Create a Session

How it works

  • Create A Session

    Go to create a session.We will prepare your personal session for you in a few seconds.

  • Login With WEB 3.0 WALLET

    We currently support two Web 3.0 wallets, "MetaMask" and "Trust Wallet". Please make sure you have installed your wallet extention in your browser. Otherwise you can also log in manually.

  • Send Session Link To Your Friend

    After your login you will receive a personal session link (only share this with your friend). The session code always has five digits. Now your friend can log into the session with you.

  • Exchange Your NFTS In A Safe And Cheap Way

    If your friend is in your session, your wallet will open and you can trade your NFTs in real time. We do not deduct commissions in your session. You share the fees with your friend. That's all!
