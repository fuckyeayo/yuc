FAQ

frequently asked questions

  • How did NFT-Sweeper come about?

    NFT-SWEEPER came about when we realized that you cannot exchange NFTs for NFTs on any trading platform. We also enable you to exchange coins for NFTs easily and cheaply. The fees will be shared between you and your friend.

  • How does NFT trading work?

    The NFTS or coins are sent to a group pool and from there the selected NFTS or coins are exchanged between you and your friend.

  • Which web 3.0 wallets do you support?

    We currently support two Web 3.0 wallets, "MetaMask" and "Trust Wallet". Please make sure you have installed your wallet extention in your browser. Otherwise you can also log in manually.

  • How to contact you?

    Please only contact us via our contact form. As we are still in beta, our website and socials are still under construction.
